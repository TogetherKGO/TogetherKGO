About Community Resources

Find grants, toolkits, directories, certification supports, and other community resources that can help residents and grassroots groups start projects, access funding, and support local initiatives in the Kingston-Galloway-Orton Park and Toronto area.

Want to contribute?

Click the "Submit a Resource" button below to share a grant, toolkit, directory, certification support, or other helpful resource. We ask that submissions follow our Terms of Service and community guidelines. Note that submissions are first moderated, then added to a scheduled update that occurs bi-weekly on Mondays and Thursdays.

Need Help?

If you're unsure how to access a resource or apply for a program, visit the official website linked in the listing or contact the organization directly for more information
