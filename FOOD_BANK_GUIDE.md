## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does approval take?
Usually within 24 hours, sometimes faster for urgent notices.

### Can I edit my post after submitting?
Not directly. Contact the admin team if you need changes.

### Can I delete a post?
Contact the admin team and they can remove it for you.

### What if I make a mistake?
Just submit a new update with the correct information.

### Can I add photos?
Currently, the basic form doesn't support photos. Contact admin if you have an important image.

### Who can see my submissions?
Only admin team can see submissions before approval. After approval, the entire community can see them on the Community Board.

### What happens if my post is rejected?
The admin team will contact you via email to explain why and help you resubmit.
